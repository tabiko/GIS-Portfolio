    GIS PORTFOLIO



    Tsunami Inundation Maps, San Luis Obispo County

    This is a series of maps that was a part of a tsunami event awareness campaign for residents living along the coast of San Luis Obispo County. Sponsored by CAL FIRE San Luis Obispo, the San Luis Obispo County Office of Emergency Services, and the County Sherriff's Office, this map was the inset of a large, six panel pamphlet. The pamphlet provided information about tsunamis and what to do in the event of an earthquake along the coastline that has the potential to cause a tsunami. Shown below are all three regional maps: Southern Region (top), the Central Region(middle), and the Northern Region (bottom).



    Beach Sand/Sediment Transport Research

    Analysis of the Morro Bay Littoral Cell and associated hydrologic sub-units. This map supplemented graduate research in geology, to identify sediment sources and to find out why there is a greater net erosion than net accretion of beach sand along this coastline.



    Naturally Occurring Asbestos in San Luis Obispo County

    This is a series of maps showing Naturally Occurring Asbestos Potential Hazard Zones in San Luis Obispo County, the Morro Bay region, and within the City of San Luis Obispo.



    Landslide Analysis Map

    Landslides and their activity levels along a highway corridor.



    Viewshed Analysis

    A viewshed analysis of the valley through Telluride, CO, including 3D visualization and line of sight profile.



    Watershed Groundwater Protection

    Areas likely to possess greater influence in the Mammoth Cave Watershed's groundwater quality.



    Suitable Grizzly Bear Habitat

    Simulated analysis of habitat in Colorado that could be suitable for the re-introduction of Grizzly Bears



    Slope and Fuel Hazard Map

    Slope and fuel analysis of Temescal Creek, utlizing NFPA Hazard Points



    Terrain Map

    A map of the landforms to show the terrain in an area of Glacier National Park. Topographic lines were geoprocessed from DEM.



    Digital Image Processing

    Utilizing MicroImages (TNTmips Digital Image Processing Software) for street and land use imaging; georegistration and resampling; and, viewshed analysis.
